Problem 2

Let \(A_n := \big[ \frac{1}{n}, \frac{n+1}{n}\big)\). Find \[\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty A_n \quad\text{and}\quad \bigcap_{n=1}^\infty A_n.\]

Problem 3

Let \(f : A \to B\) and \(g : B \to C\) be functions. Prove:

  • If \(g \circ f\) is onto, then \(g\) is onto.

  • If \(g \circ f\) is one-to-one, then \(f\) is one-to-one.
